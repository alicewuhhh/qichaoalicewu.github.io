Biography

I am currently pursuing an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Medical Imaging and Applications (MAIA), coordinated by the University of Girona, Spain, along with the Université de Bourgogne, France, and the University of Cassino, Italy as partner universities.

I received my bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineer from the University of Girona, Spain in 2022. During that journey, I collaborated several times with the Department of Computer Vision and Robotics (VICOROB) in projects related to using AI to tackle challenges such as skin cancer detection and brain tumor segmentation. Moreover, during the MAIA master, I did a summer internship with Icometrix. In collaboration with the R&D team, I developed a DL-based postprocessing for CT perfusion map artifacts.

Research Interests

I am deeply interested in the field of medical imaging, with a specific focus on neuroscience, particularly in the study of Alzheimer's disease and related dementias. My broad interests span across various domains such as neuroscience, neuroimaging, medical image analysis, and the application of artificial intelligence in these areas. My current research topics are about:

  • Medical Image Registration
    - intensity based brain image registration.

    Medical Image Segmentation
    - brain tissue segmentation.
    - brain tumor segmentation (BraTS 2018).
    - skin lesions segmentation.

    Medical Image Analysis
    - breast cancer detection and classification.
    - skin lesions detection and classification.

  • Artificial Intelligence
    - conventional Machine Learning approaches for classification.
    - deep Learning-based approaches for classification and segmentation.
    - transfer learning.

  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
    - synthetic image generation.
